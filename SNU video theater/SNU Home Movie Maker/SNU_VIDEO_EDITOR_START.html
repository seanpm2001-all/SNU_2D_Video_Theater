Content Reel Help

Overview

The content reel is a visual tape that shows imported media of your video in order. Each frame in the reel can contain media such as images, and videos. Music goes underneath.

You can also test your video and play it through the content reel.

Exporting help

Sometimes, you might want to use your video outside of SNU, or you just want to keep a backup. In this case, you should export your video to get a copy of it.
